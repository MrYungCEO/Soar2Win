The Problem Isn't Your Competence

After 20+ years in executive leadership and 5 years coaching successful women, I've learned this:

The biggest barrier to your next level isn't what you don't know. It's the voice in your head telling you you're not ready.

You've been conditioned to doubt yourself, seek permission, and dim your brilliance. But staying muted is costing you—promotions, opportunities, and the impact you're meant to make.

What Changes When You Unmute Your Power

Instead of second-guessing, you:

  • Walk into any room knowing you belong there
  • Share ideas with conviction, not apology
  • Make decisions quickly without endless doubt
  • Navigate tough conversations with calm authority

Instead of waiting for permission, you:

  • Take on stretch assignments without feeling "ready"
  • Speak up when you have something valuable to add
  • Position yourself for the opportunities you want
  • Lead authentically instead of copying others

My Signature P.O.W.E.R. Framework

  • P - PAUSE the Inner Critic: Using Positive Intelligence® mental fitness training, learn to pause self-sabotaging thoughts the moment they arise and interrupt limiting belief patterns.
  • O - OWN Your Expertise: Build unshakeable confidence in your capabilities through targeted assessments and exercises that help you recognize and claim your unique value.
  • W - WIELD Your Voice: Find and use your authentic leadership voice with authority, even in high-stakes situations where you've previously stayed quiet.
  • E - ELEVATE Your Presence: Step into rooms with the energy and confidence that commands respect and positions you as the leader you already are.
  • R - RISE with Mental Fitness: Navigate challenges and transitions using your newly developed Positive Intelligence® mental fitness muscles for sustained confidence and clarity.

The P.O.W.E.R. framework integrates proven Positive Intelligence® techniques with energy coaching methods to create lasting transformation from the inside out.
